Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian baked dish consisting of alternating layers of wide, flat pasta sheets and various fillings, most commonly a hearty meat sauce (ragù), creamy bechamel, and melted cheese like mozzarella and parmesan.

Savor our Classic Lasagna al Forno: layers of tender egg pasta nestled between a rich, slow-simmered bolognese sauce and a velvety smooth bechamel. Topped with a golden, bubbling layer of premium melted mozzarella and fresh basil.

Ingredients

For the Bolognese Sauce (filling):

  • Meat: 500 g ground beef
  • Vegetables: 1 onion, 1 carrot, 1 stalk of celery, 2 cloves of garlic
  • Tomato: 800 ml tomato passata or diced tomatoes, 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • Liquid: 150 ml red wine (optional) or beef stock
  • Herbs: Fresh or dried oregano, basil, salt, and pepper
  • Cooking fat: Olive oil or a knob of butter

For the Bechamel Sauce (white sauce):

  • Butter: 50 g
  • Flour: 50 g
  • Milk: 600 - 750 ml
  • Seasoning: A pinch of nutmeg, salt, and pepper

For the assembly:

  • Pasta: 250 g lasagna sheets (oven-ready)
  • Cheese: 150 g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Extra cheese: 250 g mozzarella (sliced or shredded)

Cooking instructions

Prepare the Bolognese Sauce

  1. Sauté the finely chopped onion, carrot, celery, and garlic in olive oil until softened.
  2. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in the tomato paste, then add the passata (or diced tomatoes) and the wine/stock.
  4. Add the herbs and let the sauce simmer on low heat for at least 30-45 minutes.

Make the Bechamel Sauce

  1. Melt the butter in a saucepan, stir in the flour, and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  2. Gradually whisk in the milk to avoid lumps.
  3. Keep stirring until the sauce thickens.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg.

Assemble the Lasagna

  1. Preheat your oven to 180-200°C.
  2. Grease a baking dish and start with a thin layer of Bolognese sauce on the bottom.
  3. Add a layer of lasagna sheets, followed by more Bolognese, a drizzle of bechamel, and a sprinkle of Parmesan.
  4. Repeat these layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a generous layer of bechamel and the mozzarella cheese on top.

Bake

  1. Place the dish in the oven for about 30-40 minutes until the pasta is tender and the cheese is golden and bubbling.
  2. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
